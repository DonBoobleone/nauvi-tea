# Nauvi-Tea - A Mod for Factorio Space Age

A flavorful, quality-focused tea production chain exclusive to Nauvis.
A locally sourced alternative to Bioflux.

Biters love it so much they chomp the barrels whole.

## Tea Plant
- Grows only on Nauvis grasslands
- Synthetic seed 100% reusable after harvest.

## Processing and recipes
- Plant & Harvest - Agricultural Towers do the trick.
- Wither - Wait or speed up the process with machines.
- Crush - Break up the leaves to start oxidation.
- Oxidize - Let it breathe in fresh air or speed up with thruster-oxidizer.
- Steam - Lock in the flavor with a quick heat treatment.
- Dry - Let it dry or quick-dry it with smelters.
- Bag - Store it indefinitely in carbon fiber sacks.
- Brew - By the barrel, from dried leaves or tea bags.

## Usage
- The premium quali-tea is meant for the Engineer.
- The rest is for feeding the Biters.

## 2 hidden achievements
- LOTR-inspired puns for dedicated brewmasters.

## Lore
Clutched in the secret pocketses of his escape pod,  
the Engineer's precious tea-bag survived the void.  

Yet to brew it proper on Nauvis' green shire,  
he left the grassy pastures for worlds of mire.  

A quest through the stars for secrets profound,  
to synthesize seeds on once-barren ground.  

Now tea plants flourish where none grew before,  
and Biters chomp barrels with satisfied roar.
