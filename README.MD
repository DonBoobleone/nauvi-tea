# Nauvi-Tea - A Mod for Factorio Space Age

A flavorful, quality-focused tea production chain exclusive to Nauvis — grow, process, spoil, store, and brew your own alternative to Bioflux.

Biters love it so much they chomp the barrels whole.

## Features

- **Nauvis-exclusive cultivation** — custom tea plants that only grow on Nauvis grass.
- **Deep, multi-step processing** — wither → crush → oxidize (uses thruster oxidizer) → steam → dry → bag → brew into capsules with steam and barrels.
- **Spoilage mechanics** — fresh and intermediate products spoil over time; careful timing and storage matter.
- **Long-term storage** — bag dried leaves to stop spoilage, then brew on demand.
- **Quality integration** — higher-quality inputs propagate through the chain for superior Nauvi-Tea.
- **Captive biter compatibility** — works as premium food; burnt barrels are consumed (no residue).
- **2 hidden achievements** — LOTR-inspired puns for dedicated brewmasters.

## Lore

Clutched in the secret *pocketses* of his escape pod,  
the Engineer's precious tea-bag survived the void.  

Yet to brew it proper on Nauvis' green shire,  
he left the grassy pastures for worlds of mire.  

A quest through the stars for secrets profound,  
to synthetize seeds on once-barren ground.  

Now tea-plants flourish where none grew before,  
and Biters chomp barrels with satisfied roar.
